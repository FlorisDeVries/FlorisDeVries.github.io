About Me

I'm a software engineer and game developer, currently working as a Product Owner at Dynatos.

My academic background includes an MSc in Game and Media Technology and a BSc in Game Technology, both in computer science, where I specialized in C# (.NET) and Unity.
My skill set also extends to C++, Python, Haskell, Kotlin, HTML, CSS, SQL, and PHP. These languages have enabled me to complete diverse projects, ranging from AI development to real-time ray tracing.

My journey in game development began with a minor in Game Design at the HKU (University of the Arts Utrecht), followed by several passion projects.

In my portfolio, I showcase projects that I’ve worked on as a solo developer or as part of a team. These projects reflect my passion for game design and development, and I’m proud to share them with you. Take a look!
