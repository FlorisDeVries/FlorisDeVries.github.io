--- id: blind_game title: The Blind Game summary: Je moet aliens schieten, maar je ziet ze niet o.0 platform: Unity logo: assets/images/projects/BlindGame/BlindGame.png --- During the game design minor we had the challenge to create a blind game, i.e. a game without visuals. Together with Ben I created a game where the player has to defeat an enless horde of aliens. The player gets dropped on a planet filled with aliens, and with auditory cues the player need to find out where the aliens are and shoot them! Check out the project here!
