--- layout: project id: car_tutor title: Car Tutor summary: Teaching my younger brother to drive... platform: Unity logo: assets/images/projects/SurpriseSimon/SurpriseSimon.png --- The second year of creating games as a "surprise" I had drawn my little brother. He just started to take driving lessons, so I had found a source of inspiration and decided to teach him to drive a car... in a more safe and forgiving environment.
