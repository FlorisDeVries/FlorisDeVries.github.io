--- id: blast_off title: Blast Off! summary: Dit was een leuk spelletje zeg, ongelooflijk! platform: Unity logo: assets/images/projects/BlastOff/BlastOff.png --- For the course "Game Physics" Niels Mulder and I implemented a simulation of the solar system in Unity ...with our own physics. To make the "game" a game we also created a level system where the player has to try and shoot a rocket from one planet to another within a less accurate simulation... Would be waaaay to difficult to play otherwise :). And as a cherry on the cake we also included a sandbox mode in which you can try and create your very own solar system!
